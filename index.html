About SRI SAI GURU SYSTEMS

Your trusted partner in systems, laptop servicing, printer setup, and CCTV security integration.

SRI SAI GURU SYSTEMS is a trusted technology and security solutions provider dedicated to serving residential, commercial, and enterprise clients. With our specialized expertise in computer systems, laptop restoration, network printers, and CCTV surveillance networks, we deliver reliable setup and repair solutions.

Our team uses certified components and advanced diagnostics to keep your computational and security infrastructure running seamlessly. We focus on rapid diagnostic turnaround and high-fidelity workmanship to build long-term trust with every client.

Our Core Services

High-quality sales, installation, and comprehensive servicing for all your workspace technology and security grids.

SYSTEMS Sales & Service

Premium desktop system configurations, office workstation setups, custom PC builds, components installation, RAM/SSD upgrades, motherboard repairs, and scheduled OS optimizations.

LAPTOP Sales & Service

Expert diagnostic and chip-level repairs for all laptop brands. We handle screen replacements, keyboard servicing, heating issues, power port repairs, battery replacements, and SSD speed tuning.

PRINTERS Sales & Service

Full support for commercial laser, inkjet, and network printers. Services include network print setup, scanner document routing, toner cartridge refills, roller repairs, and regular maintenance.

CCTV Sales & Service

Complete surveillance network configurations. We provide top-grade 4K dome and bullet camera installations, remote viewing setups, cloud storage mapping, NVR/DVR configurations, and security audits.
